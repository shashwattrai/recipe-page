Cold Coffee

To make delicious cold coffee, dissolve 1 tablespoon of instant coffee and 2 tablespoons of sugar in 2 tablespoons of cold water. Blend this mixture with 1 cup of chilled milk and 4-5 ice cubes until smooth and frothy. Pour the coffee into a glass, top it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream if desired, and serve immediately. Ingredients

  • 1 cup chilled milk
  • 1 tbsp instant coffee
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 4-5 ice cubes
  • 2 tbsp cold water
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix instant coffee and sugar with 2 tablespoons of cold water until dissolved.
  2. Pour the coffee mixture into a blender and add chilled milk and ice cubes.
  3. Blend everything until smooth and frothy.
  4. Pour the cold coffee into a glass and top it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream if desired.
